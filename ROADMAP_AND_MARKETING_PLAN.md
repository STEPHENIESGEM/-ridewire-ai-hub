### Phase 1.2: Bootstrap Climate/DAC Experiment (Weeks 5-12)

**Experiment**: 002-Climate-DAC (New)

**Inputs**:
- OpenDAC 2023 dataset: 40M DFT calculations, 170K relaxations of Metal-Organic Frameworks (MOFs)
- Tools: Meta FAIR OpenDAC ML models, Molecular dynamics simulators
- Partners needed: Climate tech researchers (Climeworks, Global Thermostat liaison), ML engineers
- Data: Carbon price curves, energy cost projections for CO2 adsorption

**Outputs**:
- Jupyter notebook: "Optimizing MOF Sorbents for Direct Air Capture Using AI"
- Trained ML model: Cost predictor for DAC systems (inputs: MOF structure, energy source, location)
- Dashboard: Real-time DAC cost-to-scale model (integrates energy, sorbent, and capex data)
- Roadmap: Pathway from $600/ton CO2 (2024) to $150/ton (2030 target)

**Success Metrics**:
- Model achieves <10% error on OpenDAC validation set
- Cost predictor identifies 5+ MOF candidates with <10% cost improvement potential
- Dashboard adopted by 2+ climate tech companies for strategic planning
- Published technical article on RideWire blog

**Timeline**: Weeks 5-12 (Feb-Mar 2025)
