### The 4-Phase Intelligence Loop
1.  **Intake & Intelligence**: Scoping the problem constraints and stakeholder incentives.
2.  **Root Cause Mapping**: Identifying the "rugged fitness landscape" of the problem.
3.  **Solution Exploration**: Simulating 100+ scenarios to find the "Best Fit" strategy.
4.  **Validation & Rollout**: Deploying micro-pilots and iterating based on real-world data.

### Tool Highlight: The Diagnostic Questionnaire
*   What is the specific point of failure?
*   Who are the invisible stakeholders?
*   What are the hard physical or economic limits?
