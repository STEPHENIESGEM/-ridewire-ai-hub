# FOUNDER SAFETY CHECKLIST

## Before Sending Investor Emails

- [ ] **LLC formed** - Protect personal liability by operating as a legal entity
- [ ] **Business bank account opened** - Separate personal and business finances
- [ ] **SendGrid account verified** - Or use console.log mode for testing
- [ ] **Email domain authenticated** - Configure SPF, DKIM, and DMARC records
- [ ] **Unsubscribe workflow tested** - Ensure compliance with CAN-SPAM Act
- [ ] **Legal waiver accepted in app** - Users must accept terms before using
- [ ] **Insurance quotes obtained** - Get quotes for liability coverage
- [ ] **Accountant consulted** - Understand tax implications of fundraising

## Before Taking Investor Meetings

- [ ] **Pitch deck finalized** - Professional, compelling, and data-driven
- [ ] **Demo video recorded** - Have a backup if live demo fails
- [ ] **Financial model reviewed** - Know your numbers inside and out
- [ ] **References prepared** - 3+ advisors ready to vouch for you
- [ ] **Term sheet template reviewed** - Understand standard terms
- [ ] **Lawyer on standby** - Have a startup attorney ready to review docs
- [ ] **Cap table modeled** - Understand dilution scenarios

## Before Accepting Money

- [ ] **Multiple offers** - Don't accept the first offer without shopping around
- [ ] **Terms negotiated** - Everything is negotiable
- [ ] **Cap table modeled** - Fully understand dilution and ownership
- [ ] **Vesting schedule agreed** - Typically 4 years with 1-year cliff
- [ ] **Board composition decided** - Know who gets board seats
- [ ] **Legal docs signed** - Have lawyer review everything
- [ ] **Funds wired to business account** - Never to personal account

## After Raising Capital

- [ ] **529 college accounts opened** - Secure your children's education
- [ ] **Emergency fund set aside** - 6+ months of living expenses
- [ ] **Accountant hired** - Professional tax planning is essential
- [ ] **Will/trust updated** - Estate planning for your family's future
- [ ] **Insurance coverage increased** - Protect your new assets

## Legal Compliance Ongoing

- [ ] **Track all investor communications** - Keep detailed records
- [ ] **Quarterly financial reports** - Keep investors informed
- [ ] **Annual tax filings** - C-corp, Delaware franchise tax, etc.
- [ ] **Employee equity grants documented** - Proper 83(b) elections
- [ ] **Board meetings held quarterly** - Maintain corporate formalities
