## 1. Securities Law Compliance (SEC Regulations)

### Regulation D - Private Placements

When raising money from investors, you're likely using **Regulation D exemptions**:

#### Rule 506(b) - Most Common for Startups
- Up to 35 non-accredited investors
- Unlimited accredited investors
- No general solicitation allowed
- Must provide disclosure documents
- State filing requirements vary

#### Rule 506(c) - If You Want to Advertise
- Only accredited investors allowed
- Can use general solicitation
- Must verify investor accreditation
- Fewer state filing requirements

## 2. CAN-SPAM Act Compliance

### Requirements

All commercial emails must:
1. Include accurate "From," "To," and "Reply-To" information
2. Have truthful subject lines
3. Identify the message as an advertisement (if applicable)
4. Include your valid physical postal address
5. Provide an opt-out mechanism
6. Honor opt-out requests within 10 business days

### What RideWire Does

‚úÖ **Legal footer** on every email with:
   - Unsubscribe link
   - Company name and address
   - CAN-SPAM compliance statement
‚úÖ **Sender verification** requires proper email authentication
‚úÖ **Unsubscribe tracking** (you must honor requests manually)
‚úÖ **Spam score checker** prevents flagged content

### Penalties

Violations can result in:
- $51,744 per violation
- Additional penalties for aggravated violations
- Criminal prosecution in severe cases

### For Founders

When raising money, consider:

- **Capital gains**: Long-term vs short-term rates

## 10. Red Flags and Warning Signs

### Don't Do This ‚ùå

- Mass email to hundreds of investors you don't know (public solicitation)
- Promise guaranteed returns or "risk-free" investments
- Omit material facts about your business
- Accept money from obviously unqualified investors
- Skip legal review to save money
- Use high-pressure sales tactics

### Do This Instead ‚úÖ

- Warm introductions through your network
- Honest about risks and challenges
- Full disclosure of material facts
- Verify investor qualifications
- Invest in proper legal counsel
- Build authentic relationships
